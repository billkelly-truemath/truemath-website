Privacy Policy

Effective: August 1, 2025

TrueMath, Inc., is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle personal information collected from or about you when you use our website, applications, and services ("Services"). This Policy does not apply to content processed through our business products, such as our API, which are governed by separate agreements.

For details about how we collect and use data for developing our language models that power TrueMath, please refer to our help center.

Personal Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect personal information ("Personal Information") you provide when you create an account, communicate with us, or interact with our Services.

  • Account Information: When creating an account, we collect your name, contact details, login credentials, payment data, and transaction history.
  • User Content: Any information included in your inputs, file uploads, or feedback when using our Services.
  • Communication Information: Details such as your name, contact information, and any message contents if you reach out to us.
  • Social Media Information: Information you provide when interacting with us on social platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc., as well as aggregate analytics from these platforms.
  • Additional Information: Data you provide during events, surveys, or identity verification processes.

Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain data when you interact with our Services.

  • Log Data: Information your device sends automatically, such as your IP address, browser details, and interaction data.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our Services, including content viewed, features used, and device-specific data like time zone and device type.
  • Device Information: Data like device name, operating system, and identifiers.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to manage and enhance our Services. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Analytics

We may use analytics tools that rely on cookies to understand usage patterns and improve our Services.

How We Use Personal Information

We may use Personal Information for purposes including:

  • Operating, maintaining, and analyzing our Services
  • Improving and expanding our offerings
  • Communicating with you, including sending updates on our Services and events
  • Developing new features
  • Preventing fraud and safeguarding our systems
  • Managing business transactions, such as mergers or reorganizations
  • Complying with legal requirements and protecting our rights

Aggregated or De-identified Information

We may de-identify or aggregate Personal Information for analytics, research, or other business purposes. We do not attempt to re-identify such information unless legally required.

Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share Personal Information under these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party providers who support our operations, such as hosting services, customer support, and analytics.
  • Business Transactions: Personal Information may be shared during due diligence or transferred as part of a merger, acquisition, or similar transaction.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose data if required by law, to enforce our policies, prevent fraud, or protect the safety of our users.
  • Affiliates: We may share data with our affiliates who process information consistently with this Privacy Policy.
  • Enterprise Account Administrators: If you join a TrueMath enterprise account, your account information may be accessible to the administrators.
  • Other Users: When you share information via certain features, like shared links or third-party integrations, you control what information is disclosed.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access and review your Personal Information
  • Request deletion or correction of your data
  • Transfer your data to a third party
  • Restrict processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data use
  • Object to how your data is processed
  • File a complaint with your local authority

You can exercise these rights through your TrueMath account or by contacting us at hello@truemath.ai.

Why TrueMath

What becomes possible when AI can really do the math

AI is transforming professional work at every level. But there's been one stubborn gap: numbers. Not anymore.

Ask in plain language. Get a certain answer.
No formulas. No spreadsheet syntax. Describe what you need to calculate the way you'd say it to a colleague -- TrueMath handles the rest and returns a result you can stand behind.
Every answer is auditable.
TrueMath doesn't guess. It traverses a validated calculation path and returns the result with full lineage -- the variables used, the steps taken, and the logic behind every number.
Built for the moment that matters.
Whether you're in a client meeting, closing a deal, or submitting a filing, TrueMath gives you answers you can show -- and defend. The math is right because it has to be.
How it works

Four steps from question to certainty

01 Natural language input

Ask your question exactly as you'd say it. No formulas, no special syntax.

About TrueMath

Built on 30 years of calculation experience

Every time technology advances, our tools for working with numbers evolve too -- from abacuses to calculators to spreadsheets, and now to AI.

But early AI missed the mark. Language models could talk about math, but they couldn't do it reliably. TrueMath was built to close that gap -- with decades of calculation experience, feedback from millions of users, and a math engine built from the ground up for the AI age.

The result is a smarter way to work with numbers. Conversational, accurate, auditable, and accessible.

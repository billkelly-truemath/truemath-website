The problem

AI can talk about math. It can't do it.

Even the best large language models are probabilistic engines. They generate the most likely answer — which is not the same as the correct answer. In finance, real estate, engineering, and clinical settings, that gap isn't a minor inconvenience. It's a liability.

1 in 10  math answers from leading LLMs are wrong — even on structured benchmarks
The solution

TrueMath replaces the guess with a proof.

TrueMath is a deterministic math engine. It understands your question in natural language, maps it to a validated calculation path, runs the math precisely, and returns an auditable answer — or tells you exactly what's missing. No estimation. No confidence intervals. Just math.

How it works

Four steps from question to certainty

Ask in plain English. Get math you can stake your reputation on.

01 Natural language input

Ask your question exactly as you'd say it. No formulas required, no special syntax.

02 Parse & structure

TrueMath identifies the variables, relationships, and target output in your question.

03 Traverse the math map

A validated library of activities is searched for the correct calculation path — no guessing.

04 Auditable answer

You get the result, the steps, and the reasoning — everything you need to stand behind it.
